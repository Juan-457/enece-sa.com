Our story began in 2015, driven by the vocation and desire to keep growing in the production of a vegetable as essential as the potato.

We are the continuation of a family business with more than 60 years of experience in potato cultivation and commercialization.

From the beginning we have focused on producing, processing, and exporting several potato varieties. We also produce seed potatoes for self-supply and for third parties.

We have several refrigerated storage chambers for each of our clients. We believe this is an essential service to keep our products in excellent condition.

ENECE S.A. is an agricultural production company specialized in potato cultivation, always striving to continue growing, learning, and improving.

Our Varieties

View varieties

ATLANTIC

Variety for the chips industry. Rounded tuber, rough skin, shallow eyes, white flesh, good size.

INNOVATOR

Tubers are oblong to elongated, with rough yellow-white skin, cream-colored flesh, shallow eyes, and large size.

TAURUS

Tubers are large, round to rounded oval, with yellow skin, shallow and resistant eyes.

SPUNTA

Tubers are elongated oval, with smooth skin, yellow flesh, very high yield, and mid-early maturity.

SAGITTA

Tubers are elongated oval, with smooth skin, yellow flesh, large size, and late maturity.
